Multi-robot Systems (Other)

Multi-robot systems will be ubiquitous in our future societies. Our research on multi-robot systems for transportation, mobile manipulation and aerial applications are described in several other tabs of this website. Furthermore, we also have developed methods for:
- Mission and motion planning with deadlock resolution.
- Interactive display formed by mobile pixels.
- Multi-robot motion planning.
- Human-swarm interaction.
- Multi-robot learning.
- Multi-robot sensing.
